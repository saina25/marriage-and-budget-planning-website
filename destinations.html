1. Kerala - God's Own Country

Filled with inland water bodies, Kerala is known for its sprawling backwaters. Though the concept of a destination wedding in Kerala is still a novice, Kerala as a beach wedding destination is going to be a remarkable one.

Best Pick: The Leela, located at Kovalam is one of the best wedding resorts in India that can leave people spellbound in a moment.

Best Time: As Kerala enjoys heavy monsoon season, the months from September to March would be the best time to plan a wedding at Kovalam, which is undoubtedly the best place to get married in India.

Places to visit: Munnar, Alleppey, Kochi

Tips: Samudra beach would be the best choice for a beach wedding in Kovalam. It is offers a peaceful surrounding and is less crowded when compared to the other two.

2. Havelock Island - Paradise Of Andaman

Get down on knees or tie the knot amidst blue skies, clear waters and golden sands at one of the cleanest beaches in India. As far as the weather is concerned, it is mild throughout the year.

Best Pick: The Munjoh Ocean Resort is a lush and lavish property that is one of the most remarkable and unique resorts in Havelock to exchange your vows.

Best Time: Tourists flood Havelock usually from October to April which is the peak season. Excluding the monsoon time, September to May would be the best time to celebrate a Havelock wedding.

Places to visit: Radhanagar Beach, Elephant Beach

Tips: For transportation, choose ferries for lower cost and catamarans for high speed and saving time. If you’re on a budget, then off season rates are lower than what touches the sky in the peak season.

3. Rishikesh – Spiritual Start

Rishikesh is an interesting and holy paradise for exchanging vows. With River Ganges in the backdrop, Rishikesh attracts a lot of couples as the trend of wedding destinations is on the rise.

Best Pick: Located on the banks of the River Ganges, Ganga Kinare is a fascinating haven for wedding celebrations. Overlooking Rajaji National Park, it is one of the most popular wedding venues in Rishikesh.

Best Time: Rishikesh is filled with tourists all around the year but for weddings, October to March would serve as the best time.

Places to visit: Lakshman Jhula, Triveni Ghat

Tips: The hotel offers all the assistance required for a wedding but to be on the safer side, make your bookings well in advance.

4. Shimla – Stunning Ambiance

Who doesn't dream of a wedding amidst the lush-green landscapes of gigantic mountains? Shimla is one of the popular summer wedding destinations in India which offers a peaceful environment, pleasant climate and snow covered hills. There are many resorts located at this ‘Queen of hills’, which gives all-round services for a stress free wedding ceremony.

Best Pick: Woodville resort and Radisson Hotel

Best Time: Summer months are the best to plan a wedding in Shimla

Places to visit: Mall Road, Jakhoo, Christ Church

Tips: Hire a wedding planner if you plan to keep a wedding feast as they guide from end to end arrange everything properly.

5. Goa – Partying Hard

Getting married in Goa is like a dream come true for all the couples. A perfect mix of blue skies, lush greens and deep waters, Goa tops everyone’s list when it comes to beach wedding places in India. With choicest Indian wedding decorations that give a tropical look to the whole setup, Goa is undoubtedly the best place to get married in India. Goa is one of the best luxury wedding destinations in India.

Best Pick: Overlooking Arossim beach, Park Hyatt Resort & Spa in Cansaulim is one of the best wedding resorts in India and a signature venue to celebrate a luxurious beach wedding in Goa

Best Time: November to February is the peak season in Goa whereas a lot of events like Sunburn Fest happen during December to January. June to September is the monsoon season which only leaves the time from September end to late October a good time for weddings in a moderate climate.

Places to visit: Baga Beach, Arambol Beach, Bom Jesus Church

Tips: A wedding sometime between October to January would cut down the heat for guests. At the same time, months from March to May are preferable as it cuts down the rates of stays and also allows people to enjoy Goa amidst less crowd.
